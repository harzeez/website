    ABOUT UNDERGRADUATE (PROFESSIONAL DEGREE PROGRAM(PDP))

    A professional degree is a degree that prepares someone to work in a particular profession, often, but not always, meeting the academic requirements for licensure or accreditation.[Professional degrees may be either graduate or undergraduate entry, depending on the profession concerned and the country, and may be classified as bachelor's, master's or doctoral degrees. For a variety of reasons, professional degrees may bear the name of a different level of qualification from their classification in qualifications frameworks, e.g. some UK professional degrees are named bachelor's but are at master's level,while some Australian and Canadian professional degrees have the name "doctor" but are classified as master's or bachelor's degrees
